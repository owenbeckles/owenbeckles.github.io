Evergreen :

Evergreen is an innovative mobile application that caters to the needs of plant lovers and enthusiasts, offering a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for nurturing their botanical companions. Currently under development, Evergreen combines the power of React Native, Cypress, yarn, and Firebase to deliver a seamless and captivating mobile experience.

With Evergreen, plant enthusiasts can effortlessly create personalized watering schedules for their beloved plants, ensuring optimal care and growth. Gone are the days of forgetting to hydrate your green friends or worrying about overwatering them. Evergreen takes care of the logistics, allowing you to focus on the joy of tending to your garden.

Beyond watering schedules, Evergreen serves as a one-stop hub for all your plant care needs. It provides instant access to a wealth of plant care information, such as recommended sunlight exposure, ideal fertilizer types and quantities, and essential care tips. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting your green journey, Evergreen equips you with the knowledge and guidance to cultivate healthy and thriving plants.

In addition to its comprehensive care features, Evergreen doubles as an online marketplace for plant enthusiasts. Seamlessly integrated within the app, you can browse and purchase a wide range of plants, ensuring your collection is always expanding with new and exciting botanical wonders. From exotic species to trendy houseplants, Evergreen brings the world of flora to your fingertips.

With its intuitive interface, Evergreen streamlines the management of your plant collection. Keep track of all your owned plants in one convenient place, with easy access to their care requirements and individual progress. Visualize your garden's growth, track milestones, and witness the transformation of your plants over time.
