Overview

Avocati (a Medium.com clone) is a platform for users to openly express their ideas or share their experiences with a general audience and a vast amount of categories. Users can post and publish their own articles so that any other Avocati user can read or follow the publisher to stay updated with the favorite authors.

Features

User Authentification

Users have the ability to sign up for an account by inputing the requested credentials.

Homepage

Authentificated users have access to a personalized home page that includes trending stories. They can also access other user profiles and follow them from the homepage.

Create Stories

Authenticated users can create their own stories, allowing other users to browse their work.

My Stories

Authenticated users can view their created stories all in one place. From there, they can click on an individual story to redirect them to the stories page.

Edit and Delete Stories

Stories created by an authenticated user can be either edited or completely delete any story that they have previously created.

Comments

Authenticated users have the ability to comment on any story they view. Comments created by the user are denoted by their username as well as the time the comment was produced.

Following

Authenticated users are allowed to follow other users, and also can be followed by other users.

Profile

Authenticated users have access to a personal profile which displays the users they follow and that follow them, as well as a link to all of their user stories, and an edit button that allows them to change specific infomation tied to their account that they initally inserted upon account creation.
